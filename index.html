The origin

One photo. One slide.

It started with an ordinary post. A grandmother in Japan tried to photograph a flower and caught her cat instead. Frozen mid-slide across the dirt, paws splayed, eyes to the sky.

The internet did the rest. Over 200 million views, 1.5M+ likes and 200K+ reposts, becoming one of the most-loved animal posts X has ever seen.

No team, no campaign, no roadmap. Just a genuinely funny cat the whole timeline fell for at once. The rarest thing in the trenches: a real, organic meme.
